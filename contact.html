Common Questions

Is Doodle LED really free?

Yes! Doodle LED is completely free to use with no registration, no watermarks, and no feature limitations.

Do you offer commercial support?

This is a free community tool. We do not currently offer paid commercial support or custom development services.

Can I use this for commercial projects?

Absolutely! You're free to use Doodle LED for any purpose, including commercial installations and client projects.

Will my designs be saved?

The tool runs entirely in your browser. Your designs are stored locally and never sent to our servers. Take screenshots or photos to save your work for later reference.

Can you add [specific feature]?

We appreciate feature suggestions! While we can't guarantee implementation of every request, we do consider user feedback when planning updates.

Is my data private?

Yes. See our Privacy Policy for full details. We don't collect or store your design data - everything stays on your device.
